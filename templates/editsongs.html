music_note Inserting "embed" in the Song URL

1. Open Spotify Web Player.
2. Find the song you wish to embed.
3. Click on the elipses (otherwise known as the three dots "...") next to the song and click "Copy Song Link". This should have copied the URL.
4. Paste the link in the below field - it will look something like this "https://open.spotify.com/track/3MjUtNVVq3C8Fn0MP3zhXa?si=h2ov3JYWRJ63xsDWH-eHmw" Within this URL link, insert "embed/" directly after .com/ (removing the quotation marks) so it should look like this "https://open.spotify.com/embed/track/3MjUtNVVq3C8Fn0MP3zhXa?si=h2ov3JYWRJ63xsDWH-eHmw"
