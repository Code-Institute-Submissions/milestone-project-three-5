Inserting embed in the Song URL

1. Open Spotify Web Player.
2. Find the song you wish to embed.
3. Click on the elipses (...) and click Copy Song Link.
4. Paste the link in the below field insert embed/ directly after .com/ so it should look like this: https://open.spotify.com/embed/track/3MjUtNVVq3C8Fn0MP3zhXa?si=h2ov3JYWRJ63xsDWH-eHmw
